About me
======
I'm a **data analyst** with ample experience in cleaning and maintaining noisy data sets, as well as conducting a wide range of statistical analyses and producing reports suitable to different audiences/stakeholders. My primary tools are R and Python (I enjoy doing things "by hand"), but I can find my way around most data/BI platforms.

My background is primarily academic and educational: I'm a cognitive scientist by training, and I also have a certificate in IT/web development. I would argue that this is a positive in most scenarios: however messy you think your data are, I have probably seen worse. Teaching complex topics to different student profiles has also made me a proficient communicator. Completing a Ph.D. (on time!) gave me hands-on experience with different aspects of short- and long-term project management.

If you'd like to know more about what I've done as an academic, see [this page](https://nsfio.github.io/).
